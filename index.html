A. You now have access to our Patient Portal, the place where you will upload all your wellness documents

As a reminder, all of the following forms must be submitted before you arrive on the SNHU campus

  • A completed Health History Form
  • Up-to-date immunization records
  • A tuberculosis screening questionnaire — and any needed tuberculosis test results
  • Optional: documentation of a physical exam
Note: All athletes are required to submit documentation of a physical exam performed within 6 months of term start.

Please note: SNHU does not accept medical records from the MM International Health Clinic Pvt Ltd in Hyderabad, India
B. As an international student, you will be automatically registered for the SNHU health insurance group policy — unless:

You are under a government scholarship where the sponsor purchases a mandatory, equivalent U.S. health insurance policy.  

Therefore, you should not purchase your own health insurance.   

If you plan to arrive at SNHU before classes start, please purchase interim medical insurance to cover you for that time period.
A. As an SNHU international student, you can choose to live on or off campus.

However, because public transportation is limited, we recommend you either secure campus housing or find off-campus housing in or near Manchester, New Hampshire.

Living outside Manchester will require you to have a car or use a hired car service, which can be expensive and unreliable.

Using campus housing also makes it easier to get to and from class and participate in campus events.



  • To learn about your housing options, visit our Housing for International Students webpage.
  • To compare campus housing costs, visit our SNHU Housing webpage.
  • To find out about SNHU dining and meal plans, visit our Dining and Facilities webpage.

B. Upload a photo for your student ID now, so it is ready for you when you arrive!

  1. Download the GET Mobile app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
    Note: Once you begin your first semester at SNHU, you can use the GET Mobile app to add funds to your Penman Cash account!
  2. Select "Southern New Hampshire University" as your preferred institution. Then, click “Continue as student/employee.”
  3. Sign in with your SNHU email address and password.
  4. Create a 4-digit PIN.
  5. From the main menu of the app, go to "Settings." Then, click “Update photo.”
  6. Follow the instructions.

Please note: Your photo will be subject to administrative approval and needs to follow the guidelines below.

Your photo must:
  • Be clear and in color
  • Be free of filters
  • Be taken indoors
  • Be taken by someone else — No selfies, please!
  • Include your entire face and shoulders
  • Have a white or light-colored background
  • Not show any hand signs, hats, posters, masks, pets or other people
  • Show you smiling ☺
